We've been exploring the area! These are some of our treks around the state.

Bend
February 2015

Our Bend trip was for Gabe's 30th. It's a cute town, but if you're not a skier (we aren't), there isn't much to do in the winter. It happened to snow while we we're there, which in normal usually, but this year a rare occurance. We liked our one weekend of winter and beer drinking though.

Umpqua River Waterfalls
July 2015

On our way back from Crater Lake we attempted to stop at all the waterfalls of the North Umpqua River. We failed, but saw about half. They were all amazing and very cold. Our favorite hikes days are filled with short frequent excusions. This was perfect.

Crater Lake
July 2015

Trying to find a camping site was a nightmare. We looked for 1.5 hours, then ended up renting an over large cabin. Crater Lake is beautiful though, and so are the surrounding areas. We were particularly stuck by pyroclastic desity current (the area that was scorched by the lava).

Schreiner's Iris Gardens
April 2015

The farm behind the main entrance boasts fields of irises in blocks of color. You can see them from I-5 in the spring. The demonstration gardens attached to the farm has events in the summer and non-irises.

The VanderWigs

Receintly converted Oregonians (from Arizonans) we've spent our weekends exloring the area, drinking wine and beer, & being rained upon.
